About Me

Here you'll find more information about me, what I do, and my current skills.

Hello! I’m Sathira Dahanayaka, a passionate computer science student at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), where I am continuously expanding my expertise in web development, UI/UX design, and digital creation.

In addition to my studies, I actively contribute as a content creator for IIT ESIIT and the Computational Intelligence Society, where I develop engaging digital content to promote tech-driven initiatives. Additionally, as a member of the Leo Club of Matara, I take part in community service, leadership initiatives, and impactful social projects, further strengthening my problem-solving and teamwork skills.

I specialize in UI/UX design, web development, and digital solutions, bringing a strategic and results-driven approach to every project and I’m always eager to innovate and collaborate!

Let’s connect and bring visionary ideas to life!
